# Distant Campaign Risk Assessment Framework

## Overview
A systematic approach to evaluating military campaigns against distant targets, drawing on historical precedents and cost-benefit analysis.

## Assessment Criteria

1. **Strategic Value of Target**
   - "得其地不足以为利也" (Gaining their land provides insufficient benefit)
   - "遇其民不可役而守也" (The people there cannot be conscripted or governed)
   - Is the territory economically productive?
   - Can the population be integrated?

2. **Logistical Challenges**
   - "轻兵深入，粮食必绝" (Light forces penetrating deep will run out of food)
   - "踵粮以行，重不及事" (Following with supplies is too slow to be effective)
   - Supply line length and vulnerability
   - Transportation costs ("率三十锺而致一石" - 30 measures transported to deliver 1)

3. **Enemy Characteristics**
   - "无城郭之居，委积之守" (No cities or stored supplies)
   - "迁徙鸟举，难得而制也" (Migrate like birds, difficult to control)
   - Mobile vs. fixed targets
   - Ability to avoid engagement

4. **Domestic Impact**
   - "靡弊中国" (Exhausting the central realm)
   - "百姓靡敝，孤寡老弱不能相养" (People exhausted, vulnerable cannot support themselves)
   - Risk of domestic instability from prolonged campaigns

5. **Historical Precedent Analysis**
   - Study similar past campaigns
   - Identify patterns of success and failure
   - Example: Qin's failed campaigns against Xiongnu

## Decision Framework

**Proceed if:**
- Clear strategic benefit
- Manageable logistics
- Enemy can be decisively engaged
- Domestic stability is assured

**Avoid if:**
- Territory has no productive value
- Enemy can avoid battle
- Supply costs are prohibitive
- Domestic unrest is likely

## Expected Outcomes
- Avoid costly failed campaigns
- Preserve national resources
- Maintain domestic stability
- Focus military efforts on achievable objectives

## Verification
- Confirm all five assessment criteria (strategic value, logistics, enemy characteristics, domestic impact, historical precedent) have been evaluated
- Validate that the cost-benefit analysis accounts for supply line ratios (30 measures transported per 1 delivered)
- Check that the decision to proceed or avoid aligns with the framework conditions and domestic stability is not being jeopardized
