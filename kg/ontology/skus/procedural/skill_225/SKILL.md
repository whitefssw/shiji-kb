# Defensive Camp Circle Formation

## Overview
Procedure for creating a defensive position using armored wagons (武刚车) arranged in a circle, used by Wei Qing against Xiongnu forces.

## Steps

1. **Assess Situation**
   - Enemy forces spotted and arrayed for battle
   - Open terrain with no natural defensive positions
   - Enemy has numerical or mobility advantage

2. **Deploy Armored Wagons**
   - Position 武刚车 in a circular formation
   - Create a mobile fortification
   - Wagons form the perimeter

3. **Position Forces Inside**
   - Place infantry and supplies within the circle
   - Cavalry ready to sortie from protected position

4. **Engage from Protected Position**
   - Send out cavalry probes (e.g., 5,000 riders)
   - Enemy must attack prepared position

5. **Adapt to Conditions**
   - Use weather (dust storms, darkness) to advantage
   - Extend wings to envelop enemy

6. **Pursue When Enemy Breaks**
   - When enemy retreats, launch pursuit
   - Continue pursuit through the night if necessary

## Expected Outcome
Defensive position neutralizes enemy cavalry advantage, protects forces, and enables counterattack when conditions favor.

## Verification
- Confirm the wagon circle perimeter is complete with no exploitable gaps for enemy cavalry penetration
- Validate that infantry, supplies, and cavalry sortie forces are correctly positioned within the formation
- Check that weather and terrain conditions (dust storms, darkness) are being exploited for wing extension and envelopment timing
