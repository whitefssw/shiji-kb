# Deep Penetration Cavalry Raid

## Overview
Tactical procedure for light cavalry forces to penetrate deep into enemy territory, strike targets, and return successfully. Based on Huo Qubing's campaigns.

## Key Principles
- Travel light (约轻赍)
- Move faster than intelligence can travel
- Accept risk of being cut off
- Live off the land when possible

## Steps

1. **Select Elite Forces**
   - Choose best cavalry and riders
   - Limit baggage and supplies
   - Include only the most capable

2. **Advance Rapidly**
   - Leave main force behind
   - Travel hundreds of li ahead
   - Cross mountains, rivers, and deserts

3. **Engage Multiple Targets**
   - Attack smaller kingdoms and settlements
   - Do not stop to occupy or process all captives
   - Keep moving toward primary objective

4. **Live Off Enemy Resources**
   - Seize enemy food and supplies
   - Use captured horses
   - "取食於敌" - obtain food from the enemy

5. **Achieve Deep Objectives**
   - Reach symbolic locations (e.g., 狼居胥山)
   - Conduct ceremonies to mark achievement
   - Demonstrate reach beyond enemy expectations

6. **Return with Captures**
   - Bring captured nobles and soldiers
   - Return before enemy can concentrate forces

## Success Factors
- Speed over security
- Quality of troops over numbers
- Willingness to take risks
- Good fortune (天幸)

## Expected Outcome
Deep penetration into enemy heartland, capture of high-value targets, psychological impact on enemy, and enhanced reputation.

## Verification
- Confirm the raiding force consists of elite cavalry only, with minimal baggage to maintain speed
- Validate that the force is successfully living off captured enemy resources and not relying on overstretched supply lines
- Check that the return is executed before the enemy can concentrate forces to cut off the withdrawal route
