# Guest Hospitality Protocol

A systematic approach to treating guests equally while providing personalized attention to build deep loyalty.

## Overview
This protocol ensures all guests receive equal treatment while demonstrating genuine personal concern for their welfare and families.

## Steps

1. **Equal Treatment**
   - Treat all guests with the same level of respect regardless of their social status
   - Provide identical food, lodging, and amenities to everyone
   - Never show preference based on wealth or background

2. **Personal Information Recording**
   - Station a scribe behind a screen during conversations with guests
   - Record details about each guest's family members and residence
   - Document any personal concerns or needs mentioned

3. **Follow-up Care**
   - After a guest departs, immediately send messengers to their family
   - Deliver gifts or assistance to the guest's relatives
   - Demonstrate that you remember and care about their personal circumstances

4. **Conflict Resolution**
   - If a guest feels slighted, personally demonstrate equality (e.g., show your own meal matches theirs)
   - Address misunderstandings immediately and transparently
   - Let actions speak louder than apologies

## Expected Outcomes
- Guests feel personally valued and connected
- Reputation spreads through word of mouth
- Deep loyalty that endures through difficulties

## Validation
1. Verify equal treatment is genuinely maintained — check that food, lodging, and amenities do not vary by guest status or wealth
2. Confirm follow-up care reaches guests' families promptly after departure, demonstrating the personal information recording system is functional
3. Test conflict resolution effectiveness: after addressing a misunderstanding, the guest should leave satisfied, not merely mollified
