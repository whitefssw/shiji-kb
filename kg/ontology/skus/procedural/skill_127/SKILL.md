# Court Politics Survival Strategy

Methods for surviving and maintaining position when facing suspicion from a ruler in a dangerous political environment.

## Overview
When a ruler becomes suspicious of a powerful minister, survival requires careful management of perception and deliberate self-diminishment.

## Strategy 1: Send Family as Hostages

When the ruler repeatedly sends messengers to "comfort" you while you're managing rear areas:
1. Recognize this as sign of suspicion
2. Send all male family members capable of military service to the front lines
3. This demonstrates loyalty and gives the ruler leverage

## Strategy 2: Deliberately Damage Reputation

When your popularity with the people makes the ruler suspicious:
1. Purchase land and property at below-market prices
2. Lend money at high interest rates
3. Engage in behavior that appears corrupt or self-interested
4. This "self-pollution" (自汙) makes you appear less threatening

## Strategy 3: Accept Punishment Gracefully

When accused of wrongdoing:
1. Accept the accusation without elaborate defense
2. Show humility and gratitude for any leniency
3. Do not argue about the facts
4. Allow the ruler to demonstrate mercy

## Strategy 4: Decline Additional Honors

When offered additional titles, land, or guards after another's execution:
1. Recognize this may be a test or trap
2. Decline the additional honors
3. Contribute personal wealth to military expenses
4. Demonstrate you seek no additional power

## Expected Outcomes
- Ruler's suspicion will decrease
- You will survive periods of political danger
- Position will be maintained without provoking elimination

## Validation
1. Monitor whether the ruler's surveillance frequency (messenger visits, "comfort" inquiries) has decreased after implementing each strategy
2. Confirm that self-pollution acts are perceived as genuine self-interest by the court, not as transparent manipulation
3. Verify that declined honors are not being interpreted as disloyalty or ingratitude rather than as demonstrations of modesty
