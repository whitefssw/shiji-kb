# Diplomatic Pressure with Respect Strategy

## Overview
A diplomatic approach combining displays of imperial power with gestures of respect and honor to bring autonomous rulers back into the fold.

## Implementation Steps

1. **Prepare the Ground**
   - Honor the ruler's family and ancestors
   - Example: Maintain and honor Zhao Tuo's ancestral graves in Zhending
   - Provide official positions and rewards to relatives

2. **Select the Right Envoy**
   - Choose someone familiar with the target region
   - Prefer envoys with prior relationships
   - Grant appropriate diplomatic credentials

3. **Deliver the Message**
   - Acknowledge the ruler's position and dignity
   - Express imperial benevolence and expectations
   - Allow the ruler to save face while submitting

4. **Provide Face-Saving Language**
   - Accept explanations that blame others (e.g., "the King of Changsha slandered me")
   - Allow the ruler to claim they never truly intended defiance
   - Frame submission as returning to proper order

5. **Confirm the Agreement**
   - Obtain formal acknowledgment of submission
   - Remove symbols of independent sovereignty (imperial titles, yellow canopy)
   - Establish tribute and reporting relationships

## Key Techniques

**Honor Before Pressure:**
- Show respect for the ruler's position
- Acknowledge legitimate grievances
- Demonstrate that submission brings honor, not shame

**Allow Dignified Retreat:**
- Let rulers blame circumstances or advisors
- Accept partial explanations
- Focus on future compliance over past defiance

## Expected Outcomes
- Ruler submits without military confrontation
- Relationship restored with minimal cost
- Future compliance more likely due to preserved dignity
- Model for other potential defectors

## Verification
- Confirm the ruler's ancestral graves and family members have been honored before diplomatic pressure is applied
- Validate that face-saving language allows the ruler to submit without public humiliation
- Check that symbols of independent sovereignty (imperial titles, yellow canopy) have been formally removed as part of the agreement
