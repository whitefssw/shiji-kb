# New Ruler Governance Method

## Overview
Based on the Duke of Zhou's advice to Kang Shu when establishing rule over the former Shang territories, this method provides a framework for new rulers to establish legitimate and effective governance.

## Core Principles

### Step 1: Seek Local Wisdom
- Identify wise and virtuous elders among the conquered/former people
- Ask them about:
  - Why their former state prospered
  - Why their former state declined and fell
- Learn from their historical experience

### Step 2: Understand Causes of Downfall
- Study the specific failures of the previous ruler
- Example: The Shang fell due to:
  - Excessive indulgence in wine
  - Listening to women's counsel over wise advisors
  - Moral corruption beginning with these vices

### Step 3: Practice Benevolent Rule
- Prioritize loving the people (务爱民)
- Harmonize and unite the population
- Avoid the vices that destroyed the previous regime

### Step 4: Establish Legal Framework
- Create clear guidelines and instructions
- Document principles in accessible form
- Example: The Kang Gao (康诰), Wine Gao (酒诰), and Zicai (梓材) documents

### Step 5: Appoint Virtuous Officials
- Select officials based on demonstrated virtue and capability
- Example: Appointing those with "驯行" (good conduct) to key positions

## Key Warnings
- Do not dismiss the wisdom of the conquered people
- Do not repeat the mistakes that caused their downfall
- Do not neglect the welfare of the common people

## Expected Outcomes
- Legitimate rule accepted by the population
- Stable governance with popular support
- Avoidance of rebellion and unrest
- Establishment of lasting administrative structures

## Verification
1. Confirm that local elders and wise figures from the conquered population were consulted about their former state's strengths and failures.
2. Validate that the new governance explicitly avoids the specific vices that caused the prior regime's downfall.
3. Check that officials were selected based on demonstrated virtue (驯行) rather than personal loyalty alone.
