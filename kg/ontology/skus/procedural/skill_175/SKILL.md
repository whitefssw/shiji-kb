# Diplomatic Negotiation Under Pressure

A method for conducting negotiations with a stronger adversary while maintaining dignity and protecting core interests.

## Steps

1. **Establish your baseline** - Determine what you absolutely cannot concede
2. **Create leverage points** - Identify what the adversary wants that you control
3. **Demonstrate resolve** - Show willingness to destroy the object of negotiation rather than submit to bad faith
4. **Demand reciprocal formality** - Insist on proper protocols (e.g., fasting, formal ceremonies) that create commitment
5. **Prepare escape routes** - Have contingency plans for extraction if negotiations fail
6. **Accept calculated risks** - Be willing to face personal danger to demonstrate seriousness
7. **Document everything** - Ensure official records reflect the true nature of interactions

## Key Tactics
- If threatened, threaten back credibly ("my head and the treasure will both be smashed")
- Use the adversary's desire for reputation against them
- Never show fear even when outmatched

## Expected Outcomes
- Adversary respects your position
- Core interests protected
- Personal reputation enhanced

## Verification
1. Confirm that non-negotiable baselines have been defined before entering discussions.
2. Validate that contingency extraction plans are in place in case negotiations collapse.
3. Ensure all interactions are documented in official records to reflect the true nature of the exchange.
