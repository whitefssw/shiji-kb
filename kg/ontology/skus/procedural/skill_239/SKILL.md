# Diplomatic Envoy Conduct

## Overview
Classical Chinese diplomatic procedures for envoys representing their state to foreign courts, based on historical accounts of successful missions.

## Preparation
1. Gather intelligence on destination:
   - Political situation
   - Key officials and their interests
   - Cultural customs
2. Prepare appropriate gifts:
   - Gold, jade, silk, horses
   - Match value to importance of mission
3. Secure credentials and tokens of authority

## Mission Execution

### Initial Approach
1. Announce arrival through proper channels
2. Present credentials to appropriate officials
3. Wait for formal reception

### Gift Presentation
- Example: Mission to Zhao for military aid
  - Initial offer: 100 jin gold, 10 chariots (insufficient)
  - Revised offer: 1000 yi gold, 10 white jade discs, 100 chariots (accepted)
- Scale gifts to the importance of the request

### Negotiation Tactics
1. Use indirect communication:
   - Riddles and metaphors
   - Historical allusions
   - Example: "A bird that doesn't fly for three years..."
2. Allow counterpart to reach conclusions
3. Maintain dignity while showing respect

### Maintaining Authority
- Keep tokens of office visible (e.g., imperial tally)
- Never abandon mission despite obstacles
- Example: Zhang Qian held captive 10+ years, kept his credentials

## Crisis Management
- If detained: Maintain composure, wait for opportunity
- If rejected: Assess if offer was insufficient, revise approach
- If threatened: Appeal to mutual benefit and consequences

## Expected Outcomes
- Successful alliance formation
- Military aid secured
- Trade routes established
- Intelligence gathered
- State reputation enhanced

## Verification
- Confirm intelligence on the destination court (political situation, key officials, customs) was gathered before departure
- Validate that gift value is proportional to the importance of the mission request
- Check that the envoy maintained authority tokens (imperial tally, credentials) throughout the mission regardless of obstacles encountered
