# Recruit Talent from Rival States

## Overview
A diplomatic procedure for acquiring talented individuals from rival states without alerting them to the person's true value. Based on Duke Mu of Qin's recruitment of Baili Xi.

## Steps

1. **Identify the Target**
   - Locate capable individuals currently held by rival states
   - Assess their true value and potential contribution

2. **Conceal True Intentions**
   - Avoid showing excessive interest that would alert the rival state
   - Do not offer high ransom that reveals the person's importance

3. **Negotiate with Modest Terms**
   - Use a low-value exchange (e.g., five ram skins for Baili Xi)
   - Frame the request as recovering a minor servant or fugitive

4. **Secure and Release**
   - Complete the exchange quickly
   - Free the individual from custody

5. **Engage and Assess**
   - Hold extended discussions about governance and strategy
   - Evaluate the person's insights and capabilities

6. **Appoint and Empower**
   - Grant appropriate authority based on demonstrated ability
   - Request recommendations for other talented individuals

## Decision Points
- If the rival state seems hesitant, maintain the cover story
- If the person proves capable, grant significant authority regardless of origin

## Expected Outcomes
- Acquisition of valuable talent without diplomatic incident
- Access to strategic knowledge and governance expertise

## Validation
- Verify that the exchange terms were modest enough to avoid revealing the target's true value to the rival state
- Confirm that extended discussions were held to assess the recruit's governance and strategic capabilities before granting authority
- Check that the recruit was asked to recommend other talented individuals to expand the talent network
