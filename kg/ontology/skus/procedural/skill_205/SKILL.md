## Overview
A framework for setting up military ambushes using deception, double agents, and staged evidence.

## Steps
1. **Identify a credible double agent**: Find someone with legitimate access to the enemy and plausible reasons for defection
2. **Create a compelling offer**: Propose something the enemy strongly desires (territory, wealth, surrender of a strategic point)
3. **Establish credibility**: Provide verifiable intelligence or staged evidence
   - Example: Fake execution of prisoners, display their bodies as "proof" of action
4. **Coordinate timing**: Ensure all forces are in position before the enemy moves
5. **Deploy overwhelming force**: Position superior numbers in concealment near the target
6. **Establish clear command structure**: Appoint a supreme commander over all units
7. **Define engagement triggers**: Set specific conditions for when to reveal forces

## Risk Factors
- Enemy may detect anomalies (e.g., absence of civilians, unusual quiet)
- Captured personnel may reveal the trap
- Communication failures between units
- Commanders may act independently if conditions change

## Failure Points to Avoid
- Do not let the operation appear too easy or convenient
- Maintain consistent deception until the trap is sprung
- Have contingency plans if the enemy discovers the ruse

## Expected Outcomes
- Enemy walks into prepared position
- Overwhelming force achieves decisive victory
- Minimal friendly casualties

## Verification
- Confirm the double agent's cover story is internally consistent and can withstand enemy scrutiny
- Validate that all military units are in position and communication channels tested before the enemy is lured
- Check that contingency plans exist for scenarios where the enemy detects the deception early
