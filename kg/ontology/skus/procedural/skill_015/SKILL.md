# Military Campaign Protocol (征伐程序)

Procedures for conducting righteous military campaigns, based on Tang's campaign against Xia Jie and Wu Wang's campaign against Zhou.

## Pre-Campaign Assessment

1. **Verify Just Cause**
   - Target ruler commits atrocities (虐政淫荒)
   - Target has lost Heaven's mandate (天命殛之)
   - People suffer and desire change

2. **Gather Legitimacy Evidence**
   - Document crimes against people
   - Record violations of proper governance
   - Note rejection of loyal counsel

## Alliance Building

1. **Demonstrate Virtue**
   - Practice benevolent governance
   - Show mercy (like Tang's three-sided net)
   - Attract voluntary allies

2. **Wait for Consensus**
   - Allow allies to gather naturally
   - Do not force participation
   - Example: 800诸侯 at Mengjin

## Campaign Declaration

1. **Address the Troops**
   - Declare this is not personal ambition
   - State Heaven's mandate requires action
   - Document target's crimes

2. **Key Declaration Elements**:
   - "匪台小子敢行举乱" (Not I who dares create disorder)
   - "予畏上帝，不敢不正" (I fear Heaven, dare not refuse)
   - "天命殛之" (Heaven commands destruction)

## Battle Conduct

1. **Formation Protocol**
   - Lead from center with symbolic weapons
   - Arrange allied forces properly
   - Maintain discipline

2. **Combat Rules**
   - "不过六步七步，乃止齐焉" (Advance only 6-7 steps, then regroup)
   - "不过四伐五伐六伐七伐" (Strike only 4-7 times, then pause)
   - Allow enemy to surrender

## Post-Victory Administration

1. **Immediate Actions**
   - Bury the dead with honor
   - Release prisoners (like 箕子)
   - Honor loyal martyrs (like 比干)

2. **Political Settlement**
   - Appoint virtuous local rulers
   - Distribute stored wealth to poor
   - Restore proper governance

3. **Symbolic Acts**
   - Cease military displays
   - Release war animals
   - Store weapons (偃干戈)

## Expected Outcomes
- Legitimate regime change
- Popular support maintained
- Stable post-war governance

## Validation
- Verify that a documented just cause exists with specific crimes and evidence of lost Heaven's mandate (天命殛之)
- Confirm that allies joined voluntarily and the formal declaration framed the campaign as Heaven's will, not personal ambition
- Check that post-victory actions include honoring the fallen, releasing prisoners, and distributing wealth to the poor
