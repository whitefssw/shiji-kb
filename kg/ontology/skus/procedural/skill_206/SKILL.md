## Overview
A method for building personal reputation and organizational capacity by consistently recommending talented individuals.

## Core Principle
Recommend people who are more capable than yourself (贤於己者) rather than those who will not outshine you.

## Steps
1. **Identify genuinely talented individuals**: Look for those with skills, integrity, and reputation exceeding your own
2. **Recommend without jealousy**: Put forward their names for appropriate positions
3. **Build a network of the capable**: Your recommended individuals become part of your professional network
4. **Accept that they may surpass you**: This reflects well on your judgment and loyalty to the organization

## Benefits
- Builds reputation as someone who values merit over personal ego
- Creates a network of grateful, capable allies
- Demonstrates loyalty to the organization over personal advancement
- Earns respect from superiors who value your judgment

## Expected Outcomes
- Personal reputation for integrity and good judgment
- Strengthened organization through better personnel
- Network of allies in positions of influence
- Recognition as someone who puts institutional interests first

## Verification
- Confirm each recommended individual demonstrably exceeds your own capabilities in their area of expertise
- Validate that recommendations are based on merit and integrity, not personal loyalty or favoritism
- Check that recommended individuals have been placed in roles where their talents are fully utilized
