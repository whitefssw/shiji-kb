# Seeking Hidden Talent

## Overview
Locate and build relationships with capable individuals who hide their abilities in humble occupations.

## Steps
1. Gather intelligence on individuals with reputations for ability who have withdrawn from public life
2. Research their current location and occupation
3. Approach them informally, without entourage or ceremony
4. Show genuine interest in their current activities and circumstances
5. Spend time with them on their terms, in their environment
6. Do not judge their associations or lifestyle choices
7. Build relationship through shared activities rather than formal recruitment

## Key Principles
- True talent-seekers pursue ability regardless of social context
- Dismissing someone for their associates reveals superficial judgment
- The greatest talents often choose obscurity deliberately

## Expected Outcomes
- Access to wisdom and skills unavailable through conventional channels
- Loyalty from individuals who value genuine appreciation over status
- Enhanced reputation among those who recognize true merit-seeking

## Verification
1. Confirm that the approach was made informally and without entourage, on the individual's own terms and in their environment.
2. Validate that no judgments were made about the individual's associates or lifestyle during the engagement.
3. Check that the relationship was built through shared activities rather than formal recruitment protocols.
