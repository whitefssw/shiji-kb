# Diplomatic Survival at Banquet (鸿门宴脱身)

## Overview
A technique for escaping a dangerous diplomatic situation while maintaining appearances and avoiding direct confrontation with a more powerful adversary.

## Steps

1. **Build alliances in advance**
   - Cultivate relationships with your adversary's advisors and relatives
   - Ensure you have sympathizers who can warn you of danger

2. **Arrive prepared**
   - Bring trusted companions who can intervene
   - Have escape routes planned

3. **Show deference and humility**
   - Acknowledge your adversary's superior position
   - Explain away any misunderstandings
   - Blame subordinates for any offenses (who can be sacrificed)

4. **Use companions strategically**
   - Have loyal followers create diversions if needed
   - Let allies shield you from direct attacks

5. **Create a plausible exit**
   - Use a natural pretext (illness, restroom)
   - Do not announce your departure formally

6. **Escape quickly and quietly**
   - Leave through a different route than you arrived
   - Travel light with minimal escort
   - Have a trusted deputy remain to make excuses

7. **Maintain the facade**
   - Send gifts and apologies through your deputy
   - Do not reveal your true location until safe

## Decision Points
- If directly threatened, use companions to create diversions
- If escape is blocked, negotiate for time

## Expected Outcomes
- Survival without triggering immediate war
- Preservation of forces and reputation
- Time to regroup and strengthen position

## Validation
1. Confirm safe extraction from the hostile venue without triggering armed conflict
2. Verify that a trusted deputy remained behind to maintain the facade and deliver appropriate gifts/apologies
3. Check that escape route was secured and true location was not revealed until reaching a position of safety
