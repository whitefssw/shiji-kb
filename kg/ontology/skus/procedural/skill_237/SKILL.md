# Ancient Commercial Strategy (白圭/计然 Method)

## Overview
Time-tested commercial principles from ancient Chinese merchants, emphasizing counter-cyclical trading, inventory management, and market timing.

## Core Principles

### 1. Market Timing
- **Know the cycles**: "知斗则修备，时用则知物"
- Track agricultural cycles:
  - 金年: Abundant harvest
  - 水年: Destruction/floods
  - 木年: Famine
  - 火年: Drought
- Pattern: 6 years abundance, 6 years drought, 12 years major famine

### 2. Counter-Cyclical Trading
- "旱则资舟，水则资车" (Stock boats during drought, carts during floods)
- Buy when others sell; sell when others buy
- "人弃我取，人取我与"

### 3. Price Management
- Grain at 20: Hurts farmers
- Grain at 90: Hurts merchants
- **Optimal range**: 30-80 to benefit both parties
- "平粜齐物" (Equalize prices through balanced sales)

### 4. Inventory Rules
- "务完物" (Maintain quality goods)
- "无息币" (Don't let money sit idle)
- "腐败而食之货勿留" (Don't hold perishables)
- "无敢居贵" (Don't hoard at high prices)

### 5. Price Cycle Recognition
- "贵上极则反贱" (Extreme high prices reverse to low)
- "贱下极则反贵" (Extreme low prices reverse to high)
- "贵出如粪土，贱取如珠玉" (Sell high as if discarding dirt; buy low as if acquiring jewels)

## Required Qualities
To succeed, one must possess:
1. **智** (Wisdom): For strategic variation
2. **勇** (Courage): For decisive action
3. **仁** (Benevolence): For fair giving and taking
4. **彊** (Strength): For maintaining position

## Expected Outcomes
- Consistent profit through market cycles
- Stable supply chains
- Wealth accumulation over time
- Reputation for reliability

## Verification
- Confirm trading decisions align with counter-cyclical principles (buying when others sell, selling when others buy)
- Validate that grain prices are maintained within the optimal 30-80 range to benefit both farmers and merchants
- Check that inventory follows the rules: no idle money (无息币), no hoarding perishables, and no holding at peak prices (无敢居贵)
