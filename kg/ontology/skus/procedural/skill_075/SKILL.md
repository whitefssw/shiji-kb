# Ancient Divination Ritual Procedure

## Overview
A solemn ritual for seeking guidance from ancestors through divination, as practiced by Zhou Gong to intercede for King Wu's illness.

## Steps

1. **Preparation of Sacred Space**
   - Set up three altars for the ancestral spirits
   - Position yourself facing north (subordinate position before ancestors)

2. **Ritual Adornment**
   - Wear jade disc (bi) on head or chest
   - Hold gui tablet in hands

3. **Proclamation to Ancestors**
   - Have the scribe record the prayer on bamboo slips
   - Clearly state:
     - The current situation requiring intervention
     - Your willingness to serve as substitute if needed
     - Your qualifications and capabilities
     - The request for ancestral permission

4. **Divination Process**
   - Consult the oracle bones (turtle shells)
   - Interpret the cracks or signs
   - Verify the result by checking written records

5. **Documentation and Secrecy**
   - Record the prayer and results on ceremonial documents
   - Store in a sealed golden chest
   - Instruct guardians to maintain strict confidentiality

## Expected Outcomes
- Clear guidance from ancestral spirits
- Documented record of the divination for future reference
- Potential resolution of the crisis through spiritual intercession

## Validation
1. Confirm the sacred space was properly prepared with three altars, north-facing position, and correct ritual adornment (jade disc and gui tablet)
2. Verify the prayer was recorded by the scribe on bamboo slips with all four required elements: situation, willingness to substitute, qualifications, and request for permission
3. Check that documentation is sealed in the golden chest with strict confidentiality maintained by designated guardians
