# Hong Fan Nine Categories Governance Framework

## Overview
The Hong Fan (Great Plan) is a comprehensive governance framework presented by Jizi to King Wu of Zhou. It provides systematic guidance for ruling a state through nine interconnected categories.

## The Nine Categories

### 1. Five Elements (五行)
- **Water**: Moistens downward; produces salty taste
- **Fire**: Flames upward; produces bitter taste
- **Wood**: Can bend and straighten; produces sour taste
- **Metal**: Can mold and change; produces pungent taste
- **Earth**: Permits sowing and reaping; produces sweet taste

### 2. Five Matters (五事)
- **Appearance (貌)**: Be respectful → produces dignity
- **Speech (言)**: Be compliant → produces order
- **Vision (视)**: Be clear → produces wisdom
- **Hearing (听)**: Be discerning → produces strategy
- **Thought (思)**: Be profound → produces sagacity

### 3. Eight Government Functions (八政)
1. Food (食) - Agriculture and provisions
2. Goods (货) - Commerce and trade
3. Sacrifices (祀) - Religious rites
4. Public Works (司空) - Construction
5. Education (司徒) - Instruction
6. Justice (司寇) - Law enforcement
7. Reception (宾) - Diplomatic guests
8. Military (师) - Defense forces

### 4. Five Chronological Methods (五纪)
- Year (岁)
- Month (月)
- Day (日)
- Stars (星辰)
- Calendar calculations (历数)

### 5. Royal Perfection (皇极)
- Establish the highest standard of excellence
- Gather and bestow five blessings upon the people
- Practice impartiality: no favoritism, no factionalism
- Protect the widowed and orphaned
- Reward those of ability and virtue

### 6. Three Virtues (三德)
- **Uprightness (正直)**: For normal times
- **Strong-willed governance (刚克)**: For dealing with the unruly
- **Gentle governance (柔克)**: For handling the enlightened

### 7. Resolving Doubts (稽疑)
- Select and establish diviners for turtle shell and milfoil
- Seven signs: rain, clearing, obscure, cloudy, overcast, steadfast, changing
- Decision hierarchy: Consult your heart, ministers, common people, and divination
- Follow majority consensus among all parties consulted

### 8. Various Signs (庶徵)
- **Positive omens**: Timely rain, sunshine, warmth, cold, wind
- **Negative omens**: Untimely weather patterns
- Royal conduct affects natural phenomena

### 9. Five Blessings and Six Extremes
- **Five Blessings**: Long life, wealth, health, love of virtue, natural death
- **Six Extremes**: Early death, sickness, sorrow, poverty, wickedness, weakness

## Expected Outcomes
- Systematic state administration
- Balanced decision-making incorporating multiple perspectives
- Alignment of governance with natural order and cosmic principles

## Verification
1. Confirm that all nine categories have been considered in the governance framework, not just a subset.
2. Validate that the Five Matters (貌, 言, 视, 听, 思) are reflected in the ruler's personal conduct and policy decisions.
3. Check that the decision-making process incorporates multiple consultation sources (heart, ministers, people, divination) as prescribed in Category 7.
