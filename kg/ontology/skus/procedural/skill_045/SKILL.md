- Proper ritual vessels and offerings

- Secrecy of the ritual content is essential
- Proper sequence must be maintained
- Connection between Heaven, Earth, and Emperor must be demonstrated

## Expected Outcomes
- Divine endorsement of imperial rule
- Enhanced legitimacy in eyes of subjects

Emperor Wu of Han performed the Feng and Shan sacrifices to establish his legitimacy as a ruler who had unified the realm and brought prosperity.
