## Overview
A systematic approach to state strengthening through internal reforms and external diplomatic strategies, derived from Shang Yang's governance of Qin.

## Steps

### Internal Reforms
1. **Establish legal systems** - Create and codify laws to govern the state
2. **Promote agriculture and weaving** - Focus on economic productivity through farming and textile production
3. **Prepare defense capabilities** - Build military readiness for both defensive and offensive operations

### External Strategy
4. **Implement horizontal alliance strategy (连衡)** - Form bilateral alliances to divide and weaken rival states
5. **Encourage conflicts among rivals** - Use diplomacy to make neighboring states fight each other

## Expected Outcomes
- Strengthened internal economy and military
- Weakened rival states through diplomatic manipulation
- Expanded territorial control with minimal direct conflict

## Validation
- Verify that internal reforms (legal systems, agriculture incentives, defense capabilities) are implemented before pursuing external strategies
- Confirm that the horizontal alliance strategy (连衡) successfully divides rival states into bilateral relationships
- Check that economic productivity (farming and textile output) shows measurable improvement as a foundation for military readiness
