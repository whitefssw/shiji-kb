# Frugal Leadership Conduct Standards

## Overview
A model for official conduct emphasizing personal austerity to demonstrate integrity, build moral authority, and deflect accusations of corruption.

## Core Practices

1. **Personal Austerity**
   - Wear simple clothing (布被 - cloth quilt/blanket)
   - Eat simple meals (脱粟之饭 - unpolished rice, 不重肉 - no multiple meat dishes)
   - Limit consumption to basic necessities

2. **Wealth Distribution**
   - Share income with retainers and guests
   - Support those in need from personal resources
   - Maintain no personal surplus (家无所馀)

3. **Accept Criticism Gracefully**
   - When accused of false modesty, acknowledge the kernel of truth
   - Example response: "诚饰诈欲以钓名" (Truly, if I feign frugality to fish for fame...)
   - Credit critics for their honesty and loyalty

4. **Maintain Consistency**
   - Practice austerity consistently, not just publicly
   - Continue practices even after achieving high office
   - Let actions match stated principles

## Response to Accusations

When accused of performing austerity for reputation:

1. Acknowledge the critic's good intentions
2. Accept that appearance of virtue-seeking is possible
3. Reference historical examples (Guan Zhong's luxury vs Yan Ying's frugality)
4. Affirm commitment to the practice regardless of perception
5. Thank the critic for their vigilance

## Benefits

- Builds reputation for integrity
- Deflects accusations of corruption
- Creates moral authority for policy recommendations
- Sets example for subordinates
- Earns respect even from critics

## Expected Outcomes
- Increased trust from superiors
- Enhanced moral standing
- Protection against corruption allegations
- Lasting reputation for virtue

## Verification
- Confirm austerity practices are maintained consistently in both public and private settings
- Validate that wealth distribution to retainers and guests is documented and perceived as genuine
- Check that responses to accusations of false modesty acknowledge the criticism gracefully without becoming defensive
