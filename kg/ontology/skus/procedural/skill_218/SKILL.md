# Xiongnu Administrative Organization

## Overview
The Xiongnu maintained a hierarchical governance system with hereditary positions, territorial divisions, and military-civil administration.

## Top Leadership
- **单于 (Shanyu)**: Supreme ruler
- **左右骨都侯 (Left/Right Gudu Marquises)**: Chief advisors, handle governance

## Four Great Kings (四大王)
1. **左贤王 (Left Wise King)** - Often the crown prince, title "Left Tuti King" (左屠耆王)
2. **右贤王 (Right Wise King)**
3. **左谷蠡王 (Left Guli King)**
4. **右谷蠡王 (Right Guli King)**

## Military Hierarchy (24 Commanders)
- **左右大将 (Left/Right Great Generals)**
- **左右大都尉 (Left/Right Great Commandants)**
- **左右大当户 (Left/Right Great Household Chiefs)**

### Unit Structure
- Large commands: 10,000 cavalry (万骑)
- Small commands: Several thousand cavalry
- Total: 24 commanders titled "Wanqi" (万骑 - Commander of Ten Thousand Horse)

## Subordinate Officers
Each of the 24 commanders appoints:
- 千长 (Thousand Commander)
- 百长 (Hundred Commander)
- 什长 (Ten Commander)
- 裨小王 (Minor Kings)
- 相 (Ministers)
- 封都尉 (Frontier Commandants)
- 当户 (Household Chiefs)
- 且渠 (Qüqu officials)

## Territorial Division

### Eastern Region (Left)
- Commanded by Left Wise King and Left-side commanders
- Extends from Shanggu toward: Huimo, Chaoxian (Korea)

### Western Region (Right)
- Commanded by Right Wise King and Right-side commanders
- Extends from Shangjun toward: Yuezhi, Di, Qiang peoples

### Central Region
- Shanyu's court (单于之庭)
- Located near Dai and Yunzhong

## Noble Clans
Three aristocratic families:
- 呼衍氏 (Huyan clan)
- 兰氏 (Lan clan)
- 须卜氏 (Xubu clan)

## Expected Outcomes
- Clear chain of command
- Efficient military mobilization
- Balanced territorial control
- Hereditary stability in leadership
