# Establish Administrative Counties

## Overview
A procedure for organizing conquered or acquired territories into centrally-administered counties (县), consolidating state control. Based on Qin's systematic county establishment.

## Steps

1. **Secure the Territory**
   - Complete military conquest or diplomatic acquisition
   - Ensure stability and suppress resistance

2. **Assess the Region**
   - Evaluate population and resources
   - Identify key strategic locations

3. **Consolidate Settlements**
   - Merge small villages and settlements
   - Create larger administrative units

4. **Appoint Officials**
   - Assign one administrator (令) per county
   - Ensure officials report to central authority

5. **Standardize Administration**
   - Apply uniform laws and regulations
   - Integrate with existing state systems

6. **Document the Establishment**
   - Record the creation of new counties
   - Note the number of counties created

## Examples from Practice
- Qin established 41 counties under Shang Yang's reforms
- Counties were created after conquering regions like Du, Zheng, and various Rong territories

## Expected Outcomes
- Centralized control over new territories
- Efficient tax collection and administration
- Integration of diverse populations into state structure

## Validation
- Verify that each new county has an appointed administrator (令) who reports directly to central authority
- Confirm that uniform laws and regulations have been applied across all newly established counties
- Check that the creation of each county is documented with population, resource, and strategic location data
