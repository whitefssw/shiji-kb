# Military Deception Through Feigned Weakness

A strategy for defeating superior enemy forces by appearing weak over an extended period to create overconfidence.

## Steps

1. **Establish defensive protocols** - Order forces to retreat and shelter whenever enemy appears
2. **Accept the reputation for cowardice** - Let both enemy and your own troops believe you are afraid
3. **Build resources during the lull** - Stockpile supplies, train troops, strengthen fortifications
4. **Allow enemy to become complacent** - Years of easy "victories" breed overconfidence
5. **Cultivate your troops' desire to fight** - They will become eager for action after long restraint
6. **Prepare the trap** - Select elite forces and position them for ambush
7. **Bait the attack** - Allow enemy to commit deeply, then spring multiple coordinated attacks

## Key Principle
The longer you appear weak, the more devastating your eventual strike.

## Historical Example
Li Mu defended against the Xiongnu by refusing to fight for years, then annihilated them when they became overconfident.

## Expected Outcomes
- Enemy forces destroyed in detail
- Long-term security established
- Reputation transformed from coward to victor

## Verification
1. Confirm that resource stockpiling (supplies, fortifications, trained troops) has been completed during the deception period.
2. Validate that enemy overconfidence is evident through observable behavior (lax formations, reduced scouting) before springing the trap.
3. Ensure elite forces are positioned for coordinated multi-point ambush before the bait engagement begins.
