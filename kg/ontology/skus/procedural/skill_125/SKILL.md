# Legitimacy Building for Uprising

A method for creating perceived divine mandate and popular support when initiating a rebellion against existing authority.

## Overview
Successful uprisings require more than military force; they need perceived legitimacy. This method uses omens, prophecies, and symbolic actions to establish divine mandate.

## Steps

1. **Identify popular grievances**
   - Assess what hardships the people suffer under current rule
   - Determine if the population is ready for change

2. **Create symbolic omens**
   - Place prophetic messages in unexpected locations (e.g., inside fish)
   - Use mysterious sounds or lights at night to suggest supernatural approval
   - Ensure omens point to the intended leader

3. **Invoke respected historical figures**
   - Claim connection to beloved deceased leaders or princes
   - Use names that carry popular legitimacy
   - Connect cause to established dynastic or heroic lineages

4. **Demonstrate bold action**
   - Take decisive action against symbols of oppression
   - Show willingness to risk everything for the cause

5. **Declare righteous purpose**
   - Frame the uprising as restoring proper order
   - Emphasize moral justification
   - Rally supporters with clear declaration: "Are kings and nobles born to their stations?" (王侯将相宁有种乎)

6. **Establish organizational structure quickly**
   - Appoint military commanders
   - Create administrative positions
   - Begin territorial expansion immediately

## Expected Outcomes
- People will believe in divine mandate for the uprising
- Initial support will grow rapidly
- Military and administrative structure will be established
- Movement will gain momentum beyond initial organizers

## Validation
1. Verify that fabricated omens have reached the target population and are being repeated organically, not just by insiders
2. Confirm organizational structure is functional — commanders appointed, territories assigned, supply chains initiated
3. Assess whether the invoked historical figures (deceased princes, dynastic heroes) carry genuine popular resonance in the target region
