# Elite Relocation Control Strategy

## Overview
A method for consolidating central authority by relocating powerful local families to the capital region, simultaneously strengthening the center and weakening regional power bases.

## Implementation Steps

1. **Identify Target Groups**
   - Wealthy families with significant local influence (豪桀并兼之家)
   - Troublemakers and potential agitators (乱众之民)
   - Those with resources to challenge central authority

2. **Establish the Destination**
   - Create or designate a new administrative center
   - Example: Maoling (茂陵) - the imperial mausoleum area

3. **Issue the Relocation Order**
   - Frame as an honor or opportunity
   - Provide incentives for compliance
   - Make refusal politically costly

4. **Execute the Transfer**
   - Move entire families and their resources
   - Settle them in the designated area

## Dual Benefits

**Internal (内):**
- "内实京师" - Strengthens and populates the capital region
- Increases economic activity near the center
- Provides pool of talent and resources

**External (外):**
- "外销奸猾" - Eliminates regional power bases
- Removes local leadership for potential rebellions
- Breaks established networks of influence

## Key Principle
"不诛而害除" (Eliminate the threat without execution)
- No bloodshed or persecution
- No martyrs created
- Maintains appearance of benevolence

## Expected Outcomes
- Central authority strengthened
- Regional power bases dissolved
- Potential threats neutralized peacefully
- Capital region economically enhanced

## Verification
- Confirm target families were identified based on actual influence and wealth rather than political vendetta
- Validate that the relocation was framed as an honor or opportunity, maintaining the appearance of benevolence
- Check that relocated families' former regional networks have been effectively dissolved and cannot reconstitute
