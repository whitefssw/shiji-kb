# Benevolent Governance Demonstration (德政示范)

Methods for rulers to demonstrate virtue and gain popular support, based on Tang's example.

## Core Principle
"汤德至矣，及禽兽" (Tang's virtue reached the utmost, extending even to beasts)

## The Three-Sided Net Method

### The Story
Tang saw a hunter with nets on four sides, praying: "May all creatures from heaven and earth enter my net."

Tang said: "This is too extreme!" and removed three sides.

His new prayer: "欲左，左。欲右，右。不用命，乃入吾网。"

### Application to Governance

1. **Offer Freedom First**
   - Allow people to choose their path
   - Do not trap or coerce
   - Only those who refuse proper order face consequences

2. **Demonstrate Mercy**
   - Show restraint even when you have power
   - Extend kindness to the weak
   - Let actions speak louder than words

3. **Build Reputation**
   - Allies will hear of your virtue
   - Support will come voluntarily
   - "诸侯闻之，曰：汤德至矣"

## Additional Benevolent Practices

### 1. Self-Reflection Method
"人视水见形，视民知治不"

- Look at people's condition to judge governance success
- Use public welfare as mirror of leadership

### 2. Appoint Virtuous Ministers
- Seek those who can complement your weaknesses
- Example: Tang sought 伊尹 multiple times (五反然后肯往)
- Give ministers real authority

### 3. Post-War Benevolence
- Distribute stored wealth: "散鹿台之财，发钜桥之粟"
- Target: "以振贫弱萌隶" (to help the poor and weak)
- Honor the worthy: "封比干之墓，表商容之闾"

## Expected Outcomes

1. **Popular Support**: "百姓以宁" (People find peace)
2. **Allied Trust**: "诸侯毕服" (All lords submit)
3. **Legitimate Rule**: Recognition as Heaven's chosen

## Decision Points

- When facing resistance: Show more mercy, not less
- When offered total control: Accept restraint
- When judging success: Look at people's welfare, not personal gain

## Validation
- Verify that governance decisions prioritize offering freedom of choice before applying consequences (three-sided net principle)
- Confirm that people's welfare indicators (百姓以宁) show improvement after benevolent measures are implemented
- Check that virtuous ministers have been sought out and empowered with real authority
